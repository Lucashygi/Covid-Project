Statistics


Here you will find some statistics about the epidemic situation in 7 brazilian states.
The states were chosen based on an assessment and data comparison from all 26 states and the Federal District in Brazil.

To be included in the analysis, some attributes were selected, like Sars-Cov-2 positive cases as well as all deaths registered in each state.
Based on that, the 7 states selected were:
