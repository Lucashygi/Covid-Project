About

This project was build intending to describe how was the Covid-19's situation in Brazil (until May 31, 2020) and elaborate a machine learning model to predict deaths based in biological and symptomatological features.


It was build during a student exchange program called PROPICIE 17. From Instituto Federal de Santa Catarina (Brazil) in a partnership with Universidad de Deusto (Spain).
