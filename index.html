Covid-19

Covid-19 is a disease caused by the new coronavirus Sars-Cov-2, discovered in the end of 2019.
The acronym means: "Coronavirus Disease 2019".
The New virus became a pandemic outbreak in March 2020, affecting more than 180 Countries/Territories, causing more than 10 million cases and 500 thousand deaths, until the beginning of July.
The Brazil's situation has been critic, with more than 1 million cases and 50 thousand deaths.
The project presents the data gathered over time on the growth of the number of deaths in Brazil, using the Machine Learning and statistic results

Espírito Santo

This state has been chosen based on its data and substantial features since the beginning of the outbreak, becoming the first state to have Machine Learning algorithms applied to.

Brazil

To present which specific symptoms and diseases were from patients who died of Covid-19, seven states from all 5 regions of Brazil were analyzed.
